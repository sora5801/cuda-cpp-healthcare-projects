> **not** real protein sequences and carry no biological identity or clinical

### Field meanings

- A line starting with `>` is a **header** (protein name); the loader keeps the
  text after `>`.
- Following lines are the **amino-acid sequence** in standard one-letter codes
  (`ACDEFGHIKLMNPQRSTVWY`). Any other symbol (lowercase, `X`, `B`, `Z`, `-`,
  digits) is mapped to the "other" bucket with propensity 0.
- There are **no per-residue labels** in the input — the *predictor* assigns the
  propensity from the amino-acid scale in `src/propensity.h`.

To regenerate (deterministic): `python scripts/make_synthetic.py`.

## The amino-acid propensity scale (the model)

The per-residue intrinsic β-aggregation propensities live in
`src/propensity.h` (`AA_PROPENSITY`). They are a **transparent, didactic scale**
in the spirit of published intrinsic-aggregation scales (e.g. Pawar et al. 2005,
the basis of Zyggregator/TANGO-style predictors): aliphatic/aromatic hydrophobics
score high; charged residues and the helix/turn breakers Pro and Gly score low.
The exact numbers are **illustrative** — the teaching point is the *pipeline*
(scale → window mean → threshold → APR), not the calibration of the scale.

## Full / real datasets

No real data is committed (the demo needs none). Run `scripts/download_data.*`
for pointers. The natural real inputs are plain FASTA files; the binary accepts
any FASTA path as its first argument.

- **AmyPro** — curated amyloidogenic protein regions, with annotations.
  <https://amypro.net> (downloadable; cite Varadi et al., *J. Mol. Biol.* 2018).
- **WALTZ-DB 2.0** — experimentally labeled hexapeptides (amyloid / non-amyloid),
  ideal for checking a predictor's calibration. <https://waltzdb.switchlab.org>.
- **EMDB** — cryo-EM fibril maps, for structurally validating predicted APRs.
  <https://www.ebi.ac.uk/emdb/>.

**Respect every license.** None of the above is redistributed here; the committed
sample is synthetic. For credentialed sources the download script prints
instructions only and never bypasses registration.

## Honesty / safety

Everything in this project is **educational and synthetic**. The propensity scale
is illustrative, the sequences are invented, and the output is **not** a
validated aggregation prediction and **must not** be used for any diagnostic,
therapeutic, or formulation decision.
